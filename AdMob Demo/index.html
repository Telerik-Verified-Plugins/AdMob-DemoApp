AdMob Plugin Demo

The AdMob plugin offers an easy way to show Google AdMob banners in your app.

You can choose from various banner sizes and positions. Support for fullscreen banners will be added in a future release.

Custom plugins don't work in the simulator and the AppBuilder companion app, so inside Telerik AppBuilder choose Run - Build - iOS/Android/Windows Phone - App package.
